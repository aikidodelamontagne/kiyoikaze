Kiyoikaze Vol. 1 - DVD

Shoden - Muso Shiden Ryu


In this DVD compilation, volumes 1 - 4, I demonstrate the Iaido series taught to me by Mitsunari Kanai Sensei between 1978 and 2004. This DVD series is aimed at Iaido practitioners interested in studying Iaido  the way it was taught by Kanai Sensei, therefore the techniques shown here might differ from those shown in other Iaido federations. These DVDs primarily serve as a reminder that I hope will be useful.


Claude Berthiaume Sensei

Technical Director of the Kiyoikaze Iaido Federation
