Make your payment online

You can use our online payment system to pay any type of fee to the Kiyoikaze Federation.


Have at hand the amount you would like to pay and enter it in the appropriate box.


Please be sure to add the following information in the Payment Specifics box

  1. •your name

  2. •the id number of your dojo

  3. •what your payment covers
